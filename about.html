About FoodieHub

We are on a mission to bring great food to every doorstep.

Our Story

FoodieHub started in 2018 in a small kitchen in Mumbai. Two food-obsessed friends, tired of mediocre takeout, decided to build something better — a platform connecting people with restaurant-quality meals, delivered fast.

Today, we partner with over 500 restaurants across 20 cities, serving more than 50,000 happy customers every day. Our commitment to quality, speed, and freshness has never wavered.

We believe everyone deserves a great meal, whether it is a quick lunch or a special dinner. That is the FoodieHub promise.
